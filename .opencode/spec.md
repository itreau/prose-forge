# ProseForge

## Overview

ProseForge is a design-oriented writing environment built for creative writers who care as much about atmosphere as they do about words. Rather than treating writing as a purely functional activity, ProseForge transforms the editor into a customizable creative space where visuals, themes, and workflows can be tailored to match the writer's mood and process.

At its core, ProseForge is a modern text editor designed for drafting stories, novels, scripts, journals, and long-form content. Surrounding the editor is a configurable visual environment powered by dynamic themes, ambient effects, and plugin-driven customization.

## Goals

- Provide a distraction-free yet visually inspiring writing experience.
- Enable deep personalization through themes, backgrounds, typography, and layouts.
- Support an extensible plugin ecosystem for new functionality and creative tools.
- Maintain fast, responsive editing performance regardless of visual complexity.
- Create a writing environment that feels crafted, immersive, and uniquely personal.

## Key Features

### Immersive Writing Spaces

Customize the look and feel of your workspace with animated backgrounds, ambient effects, shaders, and visual themes.

### Flexible Editor

A modern rich-text editing experience optimized for creative writing, note-taking, and long-form drafting.

### Plugin System

Extend ProseForge with community-created plugins that add tools, workflows, themes, AI integrations, and productivity features.

### Theme Marketplace

Install and share complete writing environments, including typography, color palettes, visual effects, and editor layouts.

### Local-First Foundation

Documents remain accessible and responsive even when offline, with optional cloud synchronization.

## Vision

Writing software has traditionally focused on organization and productivity. ProseForge focuses on environment and inspiration.

The goal is to give writers a digital workspace that feels less like a document editor and more like a creative studio—one that can become a cozy library, a futuristic command center, a rainy city apartment, or anything else that helps ideas flow.
